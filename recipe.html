Assorted Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Shredded chicken
  • Scrambled eggs
  • Fried cow meat
  • Fried sausage
  • Diced bell peppers
  • Chopped onions
  • Grated carrots
  • Garden peas
  • Seasoning
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Pour in the cooked rice.
  3. Add all proteins.
  4. Add all vegetables.
  5. Give it a good stir.
  6. Sprinkle some seasoning and salt to taste.
  7. Allow to cook for about 3 minutes while stirring.
  8. Turn off heat and allow to cool.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
